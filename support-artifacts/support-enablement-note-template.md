## How To Investigate

1. Confirm the affected user/org/workflow.
2. Gather the time window and exact action.
3. Check logs, request details, correlation IDs, or relevant data.
4. Compare expected vs. actual behavior.
5. Use the escalation path if support cannot safely resolve it.

## How To Respond To Customers

Suggested customer-safe explanation:

```text
We found that this behavior is related to <plain-language cause>. The next step is <action>. We will keep you updated as we confirm the fix or workaround.
```

## When To Escalate

Escalate when:

- The issue repeats after normal support steps.
- The error suggests application behavior or an unhandled exception.
- Data correction may be needed.
- The customer impact is broad or urgent.
- The support team cannot safely resolve it.
